Twenty One Pilots

Twenty One Pilots is an American musical duo from Columbus, Ohio. Formed in 2009 by Tyler Joseph along with former members Nick Thomas and Chris Salih, the band currently consists of Joseph and Josh Dun. Their musical style blends elements of alternative hip hop, electropop, rock, and reggae.

Band Members

  • Tyler Joseph - Lead vocals, piano, keyboards, ukulele, bass guitar, programming
  • Josh Dun - Drums, percussion, trumpet, backing vocals
